# Character Dossiers (Working)

## Reina Sato – "Protocol Princess"
- Beverly Hills-born, Kyoto finishing school alum. Runs a members-only etiquette studio.
- Conflict trigger: livestream chaos threatens her curated brand; sparks with Jiyeon push her to defend LA etiquette.
- Growth target: embrace imperfection, share spotlight with Seoul Sisterhood counterparts during charity arcs.

## Mika "Miko" Tan – "Boss of the Pop-Up"
- Cosmetic chemist-turned-entrepreneur with a roving beauty lab.
- Conflict trigger: values metrics over friendships; blunt call-outs go viral, especially when Jiyeon critiques her KPI obsession.
- Growth target: share power and mentor instead of steamrolling—ultimately co-produces the charity cook-off with the Korean crew.

## Aya Fujimoto – "Gallery Gossip"
- Art curator and nightlife insider who narrates episodes with razor wit.
- Conflict trigger: accidentally weaponizes secrets gathered at openings, feeding the rumor montage.
- Growth target: move from snarky spectator to invested friend, especially after bonding with Hyejin over media pressure.

## Yumi Aizawa – "Zen Hustler"
- Wellness creator whose ASMR meditations fund her boutique retreat.
- Conflict trigger: competitive streak erupts when brand deals overlap; her calm façade cracks during the shopping showdown.
- Growth target: reconcile authenticity with monetization and learn collaborative wellness content with Minseo.

## Hana "Haze" Morimoto – "High Vibe Wild Card"
- Stoner lifestyle vlogger with a skater network across LA.
- Conflict trigger: laissez-faire attitude viewed as disloyalty until her heart-to-heart with Minseo exposes shared family expectations.
- Growth target: realize her intuition can anchor the group and bridge cross-Pacific friendships (plus she champions the Matcha adoption).

## Seoul Sisterhood Spotlight

### Jiyeon Park – "Metrics & Manolos"
- Luxury K-beauty CEO attending the LA summit.
- Conflict trigger: challenges Reina and Mika’s branding choices; stirs the midseason breakup.
- Growth beat: learns to drop the corporate mask during the hot springs episode, hinting at future collaborations.

### Hyejin Cho – "Confessional Idol"
- Former K-pop idol turned lifestyle host documenting the crossover.
- Conflict trigger: her high-energy stage grabs attention at Festival Night, irking Aya.
- Growth beat: bonds with Aya over media burnout, champions the rumor-truce episode.

### Minseo Kang – "Deal Closer"
- Real estate rainmaker scouting LA properties.
- Conflict trigger: perceived as poaching clients, which rattles Yumi and Hana.
- Growth beat: late-night confession with Hana reveals shared family pressures; they plan a joint Malibu content day.
