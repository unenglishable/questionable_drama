# Marketing Plan Snapshot
- **Channel Build:** trailer + cast intro shorts drop two weeks pre-premiere.
- **Weekly Cadence:** episode every Thursday, confessionals/ASMR minis on Mondays, meme-able clips on Saturdays.
- **Collabs:** partner with LA food/anime YouTubers for reaction streams.
- **Community:** Discord watch parties, bilingual subtitles, merch giveaways tied to tagline voting.
- **KPIs:** 65% retention per episode, 20% subscriber lift by finale, average CTR 6% on thumbnails.
