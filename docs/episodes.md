# Episode Outline (Season 1)

## E01 – "Tagline Trials"
- Hook: quick cuts of each housewife struggling to film their YouTube intros.
- Story: Reina’s rooftop tea becomes chaos when Hana hotboxes the glam tent; Aya hints at a past feud.
- Button: group photo ruined by an errant drone, but the footage becomes the channel trailer.

## E02 – "Pop-Up Pressure"
- Mika soft-launches her beauty bus; Yumi insists on a serene activation next door, triggering turf war aesthetics.
- Confessional gimmick: anime-style power meters tracking who controls the block.
- Resolution: improvised collab sells out, but leaked spreadsheet shows someone spying on customers.

## E03 – "Gallery of Receipts"
- Aya curates a Little Tokyo showcase featuring each woman; old text threads project on walls by accident.
- Reina hires a fixer; Hana shrugs and smokes with the projectionist.
- Cliffhanger: anonymous DM hints the leak came from inside the friend group.

- **Festival Night:** Little Tokyo summer festival anchors midseason split and surprise breakup announcement.
