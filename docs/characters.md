# Character Dossiers (Working)

## Reina Sato – "Protocol Princess"
- Beverly Hills-born, Kyoto finishing school alum. Runs a members-only etiquette studio.
- Conflict trigger: livestream chaos threatens her curated brand.
- Growth target: embrace imperfection and loosen control.

## Mika "Miko" Tan – "Boss of the Pop-Up"
- Cosmetic chemist-turned-entrepreneur with a roving beauty lab.
- Conflict trigger: values metrics over friendships; blunt call-outs go viral.
- Growth target: share power and mentor instead of steamrolling.

## Aya Fujimoto – "Gallery Gossip"
- Art curator and nightlife insider who narrates episodes with razor wit.
- Conflict trigger: accidentally weaponizes secrets gathered at openings.
- Growth target: move from snarky spectator to invested friend.

## Yumi Aizawa – "Zen Hustler"
- Wellness creator whose ASMR meditations fund her boutique retreat.
- Conflict trigger: competitive streak erupts when brand deals overlap.
- Growth target: reconcile authenticity with monetization.

## Hana "Haze" Morimoto – "High Vibe Wild Card"
- Stoner lifestyle vlogger with a skater network across LA.
- Conflict trigger: laissez-faire attitude viewed as disloyalty.
- Growth target: realize her intuition can anchor the group.
