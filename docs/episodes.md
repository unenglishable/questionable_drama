# Episode Outline (Season 1)

## E01 – "Tagline Trials"
- Hook: quick cuts of each housewife struggling to film their YouTube intros.
- Story: Reina’s rooftop tea becomes chaos when Hana hotboxes the glam tent; Aya hints at a past feud.
- Button: group photo ruined by an errant drone, but the footage becomes the channel trailer.
- **A Plot – “Protocol vs. Chaos”:** Reina directs an immaculate etiquette-themed shoot, but Hana’s relaxed antics and Aya’s instigating questions unravel the tableau. Reina must salvage her image by embracing imperfection.
- **B Plot – “Soft Launch Side Quest”:** Mika parks her prototype beauty bus nearby to capture B-roll, sparking turf anxiety for Yumi whose meditation live stream is scheduled across town. Their begrudging collab tees up the pop-up rivalry in Episode 2.

## E02 – "Pop-Up Pressure"
- Mika soft-launches her beauty bus; Yumi insists on a serene activation next door, triggering turf war aesthetics.
- Confessional gimmick: anime-style power meters tracking who controls the block.
- Resolution: improvised collab sells out, but leaked spreadsheet shows someone spying on customers.

## E03 – "Gallery of Receipts"
- Aya curates a Little Tokyo showcase featuring each woman; old text threads project on walls by accident.
- Reina hires a fixer; Hana shrugs and smokes with the projectionist.
- Cliffhanger: anonymous DM hints the leak came from inside the friend group.

## Planned Tropes for Subsequent Episodes (Story Flow)
1. **Shopping Trip:** Rodeo Drive challenge with visiting Seoul Sisterhood—each group styles the other for the matsuri, fueling cultural one-upmanship and misunderstandings.
2. **Festival Night:** Little Tokyo summer festival anchors midseason split and surprise breakup announcement; a backstage heart-to-heart between Hana and Minseo reveals shared immigrant-family pressures.
3. **Midseason Friendship Breakup:** Reina vs. Mika staged like a dramatic shoujo breakup scene, with Jiyeon’s blunt critique acting as the final spark.
4. **Rumor-Spreading Montage:** Dedicated short showing the leak mutating online and weaponized by side characters, including Seoul Sisterhood fan accounts misinterpreting confessionals.
5. **Hot Springs Truth Circle:** Spa day bottle episode where both crews trade confessionals, discover mutual vulnerabilities, and agree to pause hostilities.
6. **Beach Episode:** Malibu staycation doubles as “zen content retreat,” now featuring joint content collabs that tease a possible spin-off.
7. **Cooking Duel:** Charity cook-off where the leaked spreadsheet resurfaces and forces reluctant teamwork; Seoul Sisterhood co-hosts to demonstrate unity.
8. **Finale Showcase:** Hybrid gala/live stream resolves the leak mystery with confessionals in chibi mode, ending on mutual respect and a wink at future crossovers.

## Season-Long C Plot – “Daruma Diaries”
- Reina unveils an unpainted daruma doll during E01’s tea, vowing to fill one eye when the channel officially launches and the other when the cast proves they can stay united.
- Episodes sprinkle in quick beats of the daruma being customized, borrowed for luck, or even kidnapped for pranks—adding levity amid escalating drama.
- During the finale live stream, both the LA crew and Seoul Sisterhood fill the second eye together, teasing future crossovers.
